Welcome to OK ROBOT

February 1, 2026

We built OK ROBOT because we saw a gap: too many companies talking about AI, not enough actually building it. This blog is where we'll share what we've learned — the hard-won lessons from shipping production AI systems.

What to expect

Here's what we'll be writing about:

  • Technical deep-dives — how we approach real problems with AI, from architecture decisions to deployment
  • Industry perspective — cutting through the noise to talk about what actually matters
  • Lessons from the field — what works, what doesn't, and why most AI projects fail

Why a blog?

We believe in building in public. The AI space is full of hype and hand-waving. We'd rather show our work.

Every post here will come from direct experience — no recycled takes, no thought-leadership fluff. Just operators sharing what they know.

"The best AI strategy is a team that doesn't need us forever."

That philosophy extends to this blog. We want to give you enough signal that you can make better decisions about AI, whether or not you ever work with us.

We have a lot to share. If you want to know when we publish something new, get on our list.
